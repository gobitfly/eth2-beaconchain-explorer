Phase 0 Beacon Chain

The Ethereum Beacon Chain is currently undergoing genesis. Please prepare your validators and send your deposits. To join Ethereum 2.0 fire up one of the beacon chain and validator clients. The Mainnet will support Prysm, Lighthouse, Teku, Nimbus. Please visit the official Launchpad for more information on how to deposit.

When is genesis reached?

Genesis will occur once enough deposits have been made to the deposit contract{{if len .DepositContract}} {{.DepositContract}}{{end}}. Each deposit is registered by the beacon chain after a block delay of 2048 Ethereum 1.0 blocks. The earliest possible genesis time and the estimated genesis time (according to the rate of deposits) can be seen on the right.

Explore & Manage

Once the beacon chain has reached genesis you can explore epochs, slots and see statistics. Further, you can monitor your validators performance using our dashboard feature. Sign up and add alerts on the accounts page. Get notified if your validators go offline. For more information about the beacon chain view our knowledge base.
